Powering Coastal Resilience Through Community and Technology

Samudra Sachet is more than a platform; it's a movement to unite citizens, scientists, and authorities in the collective mission to safeguard India's coastline.

Our Mission

To create a robust, real-time coastal hazard information network by bridging the gap between scientific forecasting and on-the-ground reality. We empower every citizen to become a sentinel for their community, transforming smartphones into vital tools for disaster risk reduction.

Our Vision

To build the most comprehensive and responsive ocean hazard monitoring system in the region, ensuring every stakeholder has access to timely, accurate, and actionable information to significantly reduce the loss of life and property.

The Challenge We Face

India's 7,500 km coastline is a hub of economic and social activity, but its vulnerability to ocean hazards is a constant threat that requires innovative solutions.

The Last-Mile Gap

Official warnings, while crucial, often struggle to reach the most remote coastal areas instantly. Verified, real-time feedback from these locations is frequently missing.

Data Overload

During a crisis, social media is flooded with information. Sifting through this digital noise to find credible, relevant reports is a monumental task for disaster managers.

Model Validation

Scientific models provide excellent forecasts, but they require ground-truth data to improve their accuracy. A lack of structured citizen data hampers this vital validation process.

Our Technological Approach

We leverage cutting-edge technology to turn unstructured data into life-saving intelligence.

Citizen Science at Scale

The core of Samudra Sachet is its community. By providing a simple, multilingual, and accessible mobile/web app, we enable anyone to become a data provider. Offline capabilities ensure that even in low-connectivity areas, crucial information is captured and synced later.

AI-Powered Data Fusion

Our platform doesn't just collect data; it understands it. We fuse geotagged citizen reports with official sensor data and social media feeds. Our Natural Language Processing (NLP) engine analyzes trends, identifies keywords, and gauges public sentiment to build a rich, multi-dimensional view of an evolving event.

Backed By National Experts

Samudra Sachet is an initiative of the Indian National Centre for Ocean Information Services (INCOIS), under the Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), ensuring the highest level of scientific and administrative excellence.
